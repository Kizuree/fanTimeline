TimeLine

ArcOne

Nagato was a little boy when two leaf shinobis entered his home, during a war that was taking place, and killed his family.

ArcTwo

Being an orphan, Nagato would roam around until he meets two other orphans roaming around in the war looking for food. He then befriends them and they later find a man who soon becomes their sensei.
ArcThree
After having been trained proficiently, Nagato and his two friends created a clan called Akatsuki whose goal was to bring about peace in the war.
